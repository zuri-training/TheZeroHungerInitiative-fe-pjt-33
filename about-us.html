zero hunger initiative

Our aim is to reduce hunger in our communities

We do this with the aim of reducing hunger induced crimes and killings and also to prevent malnourishment of indigent kids in the society due to lack of food and proper nutrition.

Zero Hunger hopes that in 10 years, with your help, our wonderful donor, we will be able to completely eradicate hunger in our communities.

our mission

Our mission is sub divided into two:

  • To do our bit in the sustainment development goals of our nation Nigeria.
  • Have drop off points in all local government area, close to the people who are in direct need of food.

our vision

We strive to eliminate hunger all over Africa and ensure that no one goes to bed hungry.
